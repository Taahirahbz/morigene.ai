Mission & Vision

Genomics, made accessible — one gene at a time.

Morigene was born at the crossroads of two passions: the precision of informatics and the complexity of biology. Having spent years working within healthcare, one pattern became impossible to ignore — patients are growing increasingly aware of their health, and they are no longer satisfied with approximations. They want answers. Real, exact, biological answers.

That answer lies in precision medicine, and precision medicine begins with genomics. Morigene exists to make that a reality — not just for research institutions or large corporations, but for individuals and organisations across Mauritius and beyond.

The vision is clear: to become Mauritius's one-stop shop for genomics — a trusted, rigorous, and locally rooted platform where genomic data is handled with scientific excellence and professional care.

The Person Behind It

Taahirah Goolbar

Taahirah Goolbar is a bioinformatician and genomics researcher based in Mauritius, and the founder of Morigene. Her academic journey at the University of Mauritius spans biotechnology, bioinformatics, and genomics — a combination that places her at the precise intersection of wet-lab science and computational analysis.

With a background in healthcare and a deepening expertise in genomic data analysis, Taahirah bridges the gap between clinical reality and cutting-edge science. She understands both the human dimension of health — the patient seeking answers — and the technical rigour required to find them reliably.

Currently pursuing an MPhil/PhD in Bioinformatics and Genomics at the University of Mauritius, she continues to push the boundaries of what genomic science can offer in a local and regional context.
